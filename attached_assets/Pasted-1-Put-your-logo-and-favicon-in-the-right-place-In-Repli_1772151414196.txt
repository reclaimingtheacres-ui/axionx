4) Update your /login and /signup routes to render templates

Replace your existing login() with:

@app.get("/login")
def login():
    allow_signup = (users_count() == 0)
    return render_template("login.html", allow_signup=allow_signup)

Replace your existing signup() with:

@app.get("/signup")
def signup():
    if users_count() > 0:
        return ("Signup disabled. Ask admin to create your account.", 403)
    return render_template("signup.html")

Your POST routes can remain as-is (the hashed password versions you added earlier).
