What is this demo?
🔒
Completely isolated — uses a separate database (axion_demo.db) seeded with fictional data. Your production data is untouched.
📧
All emails, SMS, and push notifications are intercepted and stored in the Demo Outbox — nothing is actually sent.
📄
All generated documents (Form 13, VIR, Transport Instructions) are stamped DEMO DOCUMENT — NOT FOR OPERATIONAL USE.
🌐
Azure Blob Storage, APNs, and LPR live data connections are all disabled in demo mode.
